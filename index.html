About

I am a software engineer with a passion for building scalable and efficient applications. I have experience in various programming languages and frameworks, and I enjoy solving complex problems.

Currently, I am a student at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with concentrations in Computer Systems and Computational Biology.

In the summer of 2025, I will be interning at Amazon as a Software Development Engineer Intern on the AWS Artifact team. Previously, in the summer of 2024, I was a software engineer intern at insitro, where I worked on the laboratory information management system (LIMS) team.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing and watching sports, especially soccer and Formula 1. I am an avid Manchester United fan and have watched every game for the last 8 seasons.
